The Crew

Your AI team for marketing & sales

AI that works alongside your team, not instead of your team. Not a command center — a foundation that makes your marketers sharper, faster, and more focused.

What is The Crew

Ten specialised AI agents, organised in 3 phases. Each with a role. Together they run your marketing from intelligence to execution to performance — with you in control at every critical moment.

The Crew works the way a good agency would work if it never lost anyone, never got tired, and never lost tribal knowledge.

10 agents. 3 phases. You're always in control.

Most agencies run on email chains, spreadsheets and tribal knowledge. When someone leaves, the expertise walks out the door. The Crew captures that knowledge and builds on it.

How it gets smarter

The Crew learns what you do. The more runs, the sharper the insights.

Run 1

Runs on benchmarks. Spots where your budget leaks and which foundations need fixing first.

Run 3

Runs on your data. Recognises patterns in your market, your customers, your way of working.

Run 10+

Knows your business better than a new hire. Predicts what works before you test it.

Knowledge in people's heads disappears.
